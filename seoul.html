About Hospital

  • SNUH is a leading university hospital affiliated with Seoul National University College of Medicine.
  • It handles over 3 million patient visits annually.
  • SNUH provides advanced medical research, education, and treatment, including firsts in Korea like liver transplantation and test-tube baby.
  • The hospital operates many branches and centres: Children’s Hospital, Cancer Hospital, Bundang Hospital, Boramae Hospital, Gangnam Center etc.

Amenities

  • 24-hour emergency services in all major branches.
  • Comprehensive diagnostic radiology, ICU, NICU, surgery and specialized departments.
  • Branches for specialized care (children, cancer etc.) and regional services.
  • International patient services and multilingual support.
  • Strong research and education programs with high academic standards.
  • Advanced surgical, transplant, and medical technology operations.
  • Support services including outpatient clinics, inpatient wards, ICU, special care units.
  • Located centrally in Seoul, with good transport links and visibility.
