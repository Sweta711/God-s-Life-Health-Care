About Hospital

  • Charité is one of Europe’s largest university hospitals, combining high‐level clinical care, research, and education.
  • It serves as the medical faculty for both Humboldt Universität zu Berlin and Freie Universität Berlin.
  • The hospital handles ~130,000 inpatient and ~700,000 outpatient cases annually.
  • Four campuses in Berlin: Mitte, Virchow‐Klinikum, Benjamin Franklin, and Berlin Buch.
  • It has a wide range of specialty services: cardiology, neurology, oncology, transplant medicine, emergency care, pediatric & neonatal, etc.
  • Active research & strong educational programs; many Nobel laureates have been associated; rich history dating back over 300 years.

Amenities

  • Comprehensive international patient services and multilingual support.
  • State‐of‐the‐art diagnostic imaging, surgical, and intensive care units.
  • Research institutes, clinical trial facilities, and innovation centers.
  • Emergency department open 24/7.
  • Teaching & training programs for doctors, nurses, allied health professionals.
  • Patient care with modern wards, single & multi‐bed rooms, comfort options.
  • On‐campus cafeterias, pharmacy, support services for patients & visitors.
  • Accessible location with four campuses across the city; good transport links.
