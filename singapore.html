About Hospital

  • SGH is Singapore’s flagship public and teaching hospital, with a comprehensive range of medical services all in one complex.
  • Annually, SGH handles over 1 million patient visits across outpatient, emergency, and inpatient services.
  • Has 5 National Specialist Centres on campus for disciplines such as cancer, heart, neuroscience, eye, dental etc.
  • Strong academic and research ties; serves as teaching hospital for medical and nursing students, allied health professionals.
  • Wide-range of specialties: emergency care, surgery, internal medicine, diagnostics, advanced surgeries, etc.

Amenities

  • 24-hour General Enquiry service.
  • On-site diagnostic imaging, ICU, emergency department.
  • Multidisciplinary care including specialist centres, allied health services.
  • International patient / visitor liaison / interpreter support. (if available)
  • Teaching & training programs for medical, nursing and allied health professionals.
  • Strong research & translational medicine focus.
  • Subsidised wards / classes (different ward types) depending on patient subsidy level. (SGH has ward classes A, B1, B2, C etc.)
  • Convenient public transport access, visitor amenities etc. (cafeterias, pharmacies, etc.)
